Hatch Your Egg

Every player starts with an egg. When you hatch it, the game generates a unique pigeon — traits, feather patterns, colors, and stats all determined programmatically. No two birds are the same.

Step 1
🏟️
Enter the Tooniseum

The Tooniseum is the battle arena. Pit your pigeon against others in turn-based combat. Win fights to climb the leaderboard and earn rewards. Lose and go back to training.

Step 2
🧬
Breed & Evolve

Two pigeons, one new bird. Breeding combines the trait pools of both parents to produce an offspring with inherited characteristics — and sometimes, rare mutations neither parent had.
